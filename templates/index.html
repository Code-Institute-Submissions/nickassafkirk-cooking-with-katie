About Katie

I'm Katie, an absolute food nut who's worked in the food and hospitality business for my entire career.

From humble beginings working as a waitress in a local caf√©, to owning my chain of restaurants, to writing my first cook book, my entire life has revolved around food.

In the rare moments when I'm not cooking, travel is my other passion and I've been lucky enough to travel to many countries all over the world and to experience the many wonderful people, cultures and, of course, foods.

During my travels, seeing how food can bring people together across cultural and language barriers, amongst peace and conflict eventually inspired me to launch Cooking With Katie.

Our mission is to create a global platform to bring people together through a shared love of all things food.
